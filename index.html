A standard employment contract typically includes:

1. Job Title & Responsibilities: Clearly defines the position and duties

2. Compensation: Salary, benefits, and payment schedule

3. Term & Termination: Employment period and notice requirements

4. Confidentiality: Protects proprietary information

5. Non-Compete: Restrictions on working for competitors

6. Intellectual Property: Ownership of work created during employment
